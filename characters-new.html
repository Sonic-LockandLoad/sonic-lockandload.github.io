Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic is the fastest character in Sonic: Lock & Load, balanced between combat and platforming. He's designed to be accessible to first time players, but he also has a high skill ceiling for more skilled players.

Sonic focuses mostly on fast FPS action, having weapons that aren't that powerful alone, but are designed to be used in tandem, for weapon combos. His movement abilities are pretty powerful, but uncontrollable if you don't know how to use him.

Skills

  • Kick - Offhand Attack
    Sonic can kick enemies at any time. Damage is doubled if Sonic is mid-air. Can activate attackable switches.
  • Wall Jump - Jump when facing wall
    Sonic can jump off of walls to gain extra height and distance.
  • Double Jump - Jump mid-air
    Sonic can jump mid-air. Wall jumping will give him a free double jump. If Sonic is underwater, he can jump infinite times.
  • Quick Step - Offhand Ability on the ground
    Sonic can quickly sidestep to avoid attacks. A Quick Step will render Sonic invulnerable for 10 tics (just over a quarter of a second). Hold down any movement keys during a Quick Step to move in that direction.
  • Homing Snap - Offhand Ability mid-air
    Homing Snap allows Sonic to home in on any attackable target if he's not too far away. As long as you keep looking at the target, Sonic will be suspended mid-air for a couple of seconds.
    You can follow up by attacking with a kick by pressing Offhand Attack, or by pressing Jump to escape.
    By default, a reticle will appear whenever you're looking at a target you can Homing Snap to while Sonic is in the air. This can be changed to be always shown or not shown at all.

Weapons

  • Caliburn - The Sacred Sword.
    • Sword Swing - Fire
      Swing Caliburn from side to side. Rapidly press Fire to perform a combo. Hits multiple times in a single swing, which can stunlock enemies.
    • Boost - Alt-Fire
      Make Sonic zoom forward at high speed. The longer you hold, the faster it'll go. Cannot be held mid-air.
    • Slam Attack - Crouch mid-air
      Sonic can thrust himself downward with Caliburn and slam into the ground. Works as a low-range AoE attack.
    • Soul Surge - Offhand Ability with Caliburn, then Fire when blade glows white
      The Soul Surge is back and better than ever! During a Homing Snap, while holding Caliburn, Sonic will be able to attack with an extra-powerful strike that destroys any single enemy. You have to attack with the right timing, so make sure you don't mess it up! Unlike in SatBK, you won't be able to use it consecutively. Costs 400 Chaos Energy. Sonic is invincible while the attack is charging.
  • Accelerated Pistol - A burst pistol crossed with a railgun.
    • Burst Fire - Fire
      Fire three bullets in quick succession. Can be spammed.
    • Recoil Burst - Charge with Alt-Fire, press Fire to use
      Charge a railgun blast, and then fire. The longer you charge, the more powerful the attack will be, and the more energy it'll consume.
  • Rapid Shotty - An automatic shotgun with an adjustable spread.
    • Rapid Shot - Fire
      Fire a wide spread of pellets. Can be spammed with the right timing.
    • Force Shot - Alt-Fire
      Fire a tight spread of pellets that sends Sonic backwards. Can be performed twice in a row, but needs to reload after firing.
  • Shuriken Crossbow - A modified crossbow that fires shurikens at high speed.
    • Shuriken Launch - Fire
      Fire a shuriken that tears through multiple enemies. Has a bit of a long cooldown.
  • Eclipse Blaster Mk. V - An enhanced plasma rifle that has the ability to heal.
    • Eclipse Shot - Fire a stream of plasma. Fires in short bursts.
    • Chaos Restoration - Alt-Fire with Eclipse Blaster Mk. V
      The Eclipse Blaster Mk. V can heal Sonic.
  • Rocket Rioter - A rocket launcher that can be rapidly fired.
    • Rocket Launch - Fire
      Fire a rocket. Deals more damage if fired while Sonic is in the air.
    • Remote Detonation - Alt-Fire with Rocket Rioter
      Sonic will remotely make any rockets he fired explode.

Amy Rose

Amy is slower than everyone else, but she has the most control over her movement, making platforming sections easier than either of the Sonics.

In combat, she takes increased damage, so prioritising defence or evading attacks is important.

Skills

  • Kick - Offhand Attack
    Amy's kick is slower than Sonic's, and doesn't do much damage. It's more useful for getting enemies away from you. Can activate attackable switches.
  • Double Jump - Jump mid-air
    Amy can jump mid-air. Unlike with Sonic, the double jump is typically needed to jump longer gaps that Sonic can easily cross with a single jump.
  • Slide - Offhand Ability
    To get through tight spaces, press the Offhand Ability button to slide! Amy's Slide will give her a sudden burst of speed, and have her height set to be low, so she can slide under gaps too small to pass through normally. She can also slide over gaps like in Crash 4. However, it does not deal damage.
  • Slide Jump - Jump during Slide
    Amy can jump during Slide to have her jump height boosted. If she jumps out of a slide while mid-air, it'll be treated as a normal jump, and she'll still be able to double jump afterwards.

Weapons

  • Piko Piko Hammer - Amy's signature weapon.
    • Piko Strike - Fire Swing the Piko Piko Hammer forwards. Has a small crit chance. Can activate attackable switches.
    • Parry - Alt-Fire
      Quickly nullify all incoming damage, reflect projectiles and telekinetically attack hitscanning enemies. Any damage Amy normally takes while parrying instead withdraws from her Chaos Energy, and if she's out of Energy, attempts to parry won't protect her at all.
    • Hammer Jump - Jump at the exact moment the Piko Piko Hammer attacks
      Jump a lot higher than a normal jump will take you, using the Piko Piko Hammer to boost Amy upwards.
  • Silent Rose - A silenced pistol.
    • Silenced Shot - Fire
      Fire a single bullet. Doesn't wake up enemies unless it hits them.
  • Precision Shotgun - A highly accurate shotgun with a precision scope.
    • Shotgun Blast - Fire
      Fire a very tight spread of pellets.
    • Precise Shot - Fire while holding Alt-Fire with Precision Shotgun
      Fire an ultra-precise, concentrated shot. Can be cancelled if you let go of Alt-Fire instead of pressing Fire.
  • Eclipse Blaster Mk. IV - More powerful than the Mk. V, but uses more energy.
    • Eclipse Shot - Continuously fire plasma balls. Press Offhand Attack to kick and make the projectiles faster and more powerful.
    • Chaos Restoration - Alt-Fire with Eclipse Blaster Mk. IV
      The Eclipse Blaster Mk. IV can heal Amy. It heals slower than the Mk. V does.
  • Rapid Missiles - A rapid-fire missile launcher.
    • Rapid Launch - Fire
      Fire three rockets in quick succession.
    • Triple Launch - Alt-Fire
      Fire three rockets simultaneously.

Classic Sonic the Hedgehog

Classic Sonic is designed for people who prefer platforming, and don't necessarily want to focus on FPS combat that much.

His abilities aren't nearly as broken as any of the other characters' abilities, so he focuses more on fundamental platforming using his abilities to assist him, and his weapons are simply faster classic DOOM weapons.

Skills

  • Spin Attack - Offhand Attack
    A quick rolling attack. If Sonic is standing still, his height won't change during Spin Attack. Can activate attackable switches.
  • Insta-Shield - Offhand Attack mid-air
    Sonic can perform a Spin Attack mid-air. This move will temporarily turn Sonic invincible, and give him a short hop. Can activate attackable switches.
  • Sonic Thok - Offhand Ability or Jump twice (Offhand before v1.4) + optionally any movement keys
    Sonic can thok, flinging himself forwards at high speed.

Weapons

  • Unarmed - While unarmed, Sonic gains access to his Drift and Spin Dash abilities.
    • Drift - Alt-Fire unarmed
      Sonic can skid across the floor at high speed, generating Chaos Energy. If he uses it in the air, it'll be a lot slower.
    • Spin Dash - Hold Fire to charge, then release
      The signature technique. Sonic can charge up a high-speed spin attack and send himself barrelling towards any enemy who ever looked at him funny. You can press Jump during a Spin Dash to Spin Dash Jump, and you can hold Move Back to slow down the Spin Dash. Can activate attackable switches.
    • Drop Dash - Hold Fire mid-air until you hit the ground
      An instant Spin Dash charged in the air. It isn't able to travel as fast as the Spin Dash does, but otherwise it has the exact same properties as the Spin Dash.
    • Wall Dash - Hold Fire against a wall after jumping
      An instant Spin Dash charged in the air. It isn't able to travel as fast as the Spin Dash does, but otherwise it has the exact same properties as the Spin Dash.
  • Chaos Blaster - A plasma rifle with fast projectiles. Quite overpowered. Please nerf.
    • Plasma Stream - Fire Fire a stream of plasma projectiles.
  • Speedy Shotgun - An automatic shotgun with a burst fire.
    • Speedy Shot - Fire
      Fire a rapid shotgun blast.
    • Burst Shot - Alt-Fire
      Fire a quintuple burst fire.
  • Chaos Rain - A turbo-charged chaingun with a wide spread.
    • Bullet Rain - Fire
      Fire a barrage of incredibly inaccurate bullets.
  • Rocket Stream - A rapid fire rocket launcher that can be used for rocket jumping.
    • Stream Launch - Fire
      Fire a stream of rockets that launch targets far, including Sonic. Sonic takes less damage from his own rockets, so you can get away with rocket jumping.

Shadow the Hedgehog

Shadow is fundamentally similar to Sonic, but having a jump height higher than everyone else's.

Shadow's combat is a lot simpler than the rest of the cast, and doesn't have nearly as much depth as Sonic's, but he can typically get through most combat encounters easier than everyone else, since his weapons and abilities are the most powerful among the cast. Shadow is a good pick for beginners.

Skills

  • Punch - Offhand Attack when close to a target
    Shadow can destroy most enemies with a single punch. Can activate attackable switches.
  • Chaos Spear - Offhand Attack
    Shadow can fire a Chaos Spear that homes in on targets. Can activate attackable switches.
  • Chaos Snap - Offhand Ability
    A less powerful Chaos Control that lets Shadow teleport. Can only be used twice mid-air. If Shadow is holding the Chaos Emerald, he'll gain an additional Chaos Snap. Shadow is invincible during this technique.

Weapons

  • High Shovel - The ultimate symbol of respect. The High Shovel deals a powerful blow to whatever enemy Shadow hits with it. Can activate attackable switches.
    • Shovel Smash - Fire
      Swing the High Shovel forward and destroy anything unlucky enough to be in your way.
  • Troll Slaiying Knife - They cant troll you if there dead! The ultimate troll slaiying weapon, which deals enough damage to one-shot basically every enemy in the game. Can activate attackable switches.
    • Ultimate Stab - Fire
      Strike with the Troll Slaiying Knife, which kills ANYTHING it touches.
  • Sub-Machine Gun - An MP5. No, that's it. It has no special properties whatsoever.
    • Rapid Fire - Fire
      Rapidly fire the SMG.
  • Dark Assaulter - An automatic shotgun. We really like those, huh?
    • Assault Shot - Fire
      Fire shotgun blasts.
  • Quartenary Decimator - A quad-barreled, super powerful shotgun with quite a bit of recoil.
    • Decimation Blast - Fire
      Fire an ungodly amount of pellets.
  • Chaos Emerald - A Chaos Emerald replica capable of firing BFG projectiles.
    • Chaos Blast - Fire
      Fire a BFG blast.
    • Chaos Restoration - Alt-Fire with Chaos Emerald
      The Chaos Emerald replica can heal Shadow, and heals a lot faster than Sonic's Eclipse Blaster Mk. V does.
  • Dual Assimilator - A dual rocket launcher that can NOT be used for rocket jumping. Don't even try. Please.
    • Targeted Launch - Fire
      Fire two auto-targeted missiles. DO NOT FIRE THIS AT A CLOSE RANGE.
    • Untargeted Launch - Alt-Fire
      Fire two non-targeted missiles that just go straight forward. Can still absolutely destroy you if you aren't careful.
