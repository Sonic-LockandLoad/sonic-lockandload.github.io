Characters

Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic is the fastest character in Sonic: Lock & Load, being accessible to first time players but having a high skill ceiling. Sonic's abilities revolve around fast movement and high agility, but he isn't as easy to control as the others.

Weapons

  • Caliburn - The Sacred Sword. Hits multiple times in a single swing, which can stunlock enemies.
  • Accelerated Pistol - A rapid-fire pistol crossed with a railgun.
  • Rapid Shotty - An automatic shotgun with an adjustable spread.
  • Shuriken Crossbow - A modified crossbow that fires shurikens at high speed.
  • Eclipse Blaster Mk. V - A devastatingly powerful plasma rifle that has the ability to heal.
  • Rocket Rioter - A rocket launcher that can be rapidly fired.

Skills and Abilities

  • Kick - Sonic can kick enemies at any time.
  • Boost - Sonic can Boost while holding Caliburn to zoom around the place. Just be careful of walls.
  • Wall Jump - Sonic can jump off of walls. Powerful, but can be hard to control.
  • Double Jump - Sonic can jump mid-air. Wall jumping will give him a free double jump.
  • Recoil Burst - Sonic can use the railgun fire mode of his Accelerated Pistol to be sent flying backwards.
  • Force Shot - Ditto, but using the Rapid Shotty's tight spread fire mode.
  • Chaos Restoration - The Eclipse Blaster Mk. V can heal Sonic.

Amy Rose

Amy is slower than everyone else, relying more on her weapons. She takes increased damage from enemy attacks, so avoiding damage is important.

Weapons

  • Piko Piko Hammer - Amy's signature weapon. Amy can Hammer Jump to send herself flying around the arena.
  • Silent Rose - A silenced pistol. Doesn't wake up enemies unless it hits them.
  • Precision Shotgun - A highly accurate shotgun with a precision scope.
  • Eclipse Blaster Mk. IV - More powerful than the Mk. V, but uses more energy and fires slower.
  • Rapid Missiles - A rapid-fire missile launcher.

Skills and Abilities

  • Kick - Amy's kick is slower than Sonic's, and doesn't do as much damage.
  • Double Jump - Amy can jump mid-air.
  • Guard - Amy can guard using her Piko Piko Hammer.
  • Hammer Jump - Hammer Jumping lets Amy fly around the arena. Mastery of the Hammer Jump can make Amy's speed on par with Sonic.
  • Chaos Restoration - The Eclipse Blaster Mk. IV can heal Amy.

Classic Sonic the Hedgehog

Classic Sonic is aimed more towards beginners, lacking his Modern counterpart's powerful abilities, but with abilities and a gameplay style of his own. He typically handles better than Modern Sonic, but can still be uncontrollable at times.

Weapons

  • Punch - Yes. Classic Sonic can punch enemies.
  • Chaos Blaster - A plasma rifle with fast projectiles. Quite overpowered.
  • Speedy Shotgun - An automatic shotgun with a burst fire.
  • Chaos Rain - A turbo-charged chaingun.
  • Rocket Stream - A rapid fire rocket launcher that can be used for rocket jumping.

Skills and Abilities

  • Sonic Thok - Classic Sonic can thok to gain speed. Someone say SRB2?
  • Drift - Classic Sonic can skid across the floor at high speed, generating Chaos Energy. If he uses it in the air, it'll be a lot slower.
  • Spin Dash - The signature technique. Classic Sonic can charge up a high-speed spin attack and send himself barrelling towards any enemy who ever looked at him funny. It can be used to quickly change directions on a whim, and can also be used for Spin Dash jumping.

Shadow the Hedgehog

Shadow has the same base speed as Sonic, as well as the ability to teleport around the arena. His weapons are the most powerful in the game, but use up the most energy. He's typically aimed at people who want to blow everything up.

Weapons

  • Punch - Shadow can destroy most enemies with a single punch.
  • High Shovel - The ultimate symbol of respect.
  • Troll Slaiying Knife - They cant troll you if there dead!
  • Dark Assaulter - An automatic shotgun. We really like those, huh?
  • Quartenary Decimator - A quad-barreled shotgun.
  • Chaos Emerald - A Chaos Emerald replica that can fire a BFG blast.
  • Dual Assimilator - A dual rocket launcher that can NOT be used for rocket jumping. Don't even try.

Skills and Abilities

  • Chaos Spear - While unarmed, Shadow can fire a Chaos Spear that homes in on targets.
  • Chaos Snap - A less powerful Chaos Control that lets Shadow teleport. Can only be used twice mid-air.
  • Chaos Restoration - The Chaos Emerald can heal Shadow.
