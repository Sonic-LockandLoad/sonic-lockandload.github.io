DLC

Download some microtransaction-free, no-cost, no-BS DLC to extend or enhance your Sonic: Lock & Load experience!

Run any downloaded WADs/PK3s with Sonic: Lock & Load. ZDL makes this easy.

These will be updated more frequently than actual S:L&L releases.

Maps

v1.0 Green Hill

2022-08-31 - Versions v1.3 and above

Fancy a little nostalgic challenge? This DLC includes the old Green Hill from S:L&L v1.0-v1.2 as a separate episode for newer versions.

Sunset Gardens with Pizza Time

2023-06-01 - Versions v1.4 and above

This modified version of Sunset Gardens has you run back to the start after reaching the end, in a similar fashion to Pizza Tower! Are you ready to get funky?

Music

His World on Title Screen

2022-08-31 - All versions

The title screen music will be replaced with His World, like how it was in versions v0.8 and v0.9.

Origins Super Theme for Classic Sonic

2022-08-31 - Versions v1.0 and above

Feel the power of Sonic 4 Episode III with this Super Sonic theme! Replaces Classic Sonic's Super theme with the one from Sonic Origins' version of Sonic 3 & Knuckles.

Sonic Frontiers-inspired Title Screen

2022-08-31 - Versions v1.3 and above, v1.4 or above recommended

The title screen music will be replaced with I'm Here, and the title map will be Hire This Zone.

Sonic Frontiers Super/Hyper Music

2022-09-07 - Versions v1.0 and above, v1.3 or above recommended

Funk the pain away with this DLC that replaces Modern Sonic's Super theme with (the censored version of) Vandalize and his Hyper theme with I'm Here from Sonic Front Ears!

Scripts

v1.4 Physics

2022-09-01 - All versions prior to v1.4

This DLC takes the same physics code from v1.4 and applies it to your older S:L&L version.

You'll come to a stop faster on ground, you have a good amount of mid-air control and releasing all movement keys will preserve your current momentum. You'll also jump higher if playing with versions prior to v1.3.

Sprites

Modern Amy

2022-08-31 - Versions v1.0 and above

Not a Classic Amy fan? This DLC replaces Amy's face sprites with her old v0.9 sprites that look more like Modern Amy.

This is not a recreation or remaster of her v0.9 sprites -- this is simply a port and will look a bit different to the sprites used in v1.3 and above.
