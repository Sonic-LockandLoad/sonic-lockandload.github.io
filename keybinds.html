Key Bindings

Keyboard/Mouse

An overview of keybindings for Sonic: Lock & Load. Includes bindings for both keyboard/mouse, and game controllers.

It's important to note Sonic: Lock & Load was designed for keyboard and mouse first and foremost – but controller bindings are still here because there are a lot of people who play on a controller, especially Steam Deck users.

Familiar

The default keybindings, based on WASD keys. This is recommended for most players, including people used to first person games.

These keybinds are called "Familiar" because they're familiar for most players, but don't let you take full advantage of the game's controls.

Movement

  • Move Forward - W
  • Move Back - S
  • Strafe Left - A
  • Strafe Right - D
  • Jump - Space
  • Crouch - Control

Actions

  • Fire - Left Mouse
  • Alt-Fire - Right Mouse
  • Offhand Attack - Q
  • Offhand Ability - Middle Mouse
  • Offhand Ability (alt.)* - Shift
  • Interact - E
  • Super Form - Y
  • Taunt - T

Weapons

  • Last Used Weapon - F
  • Previous Weapon - Scroll Up
  • Next Weapon - Scroll Down
  • Melee - 1
  • Weapon 1 - 2
  • Weapon 2 - 3
  • Weapon 3 - 4
  • Weapon 4 - 5

Elite

The keybindings the creator uses, based on ESDF keys (WASD shifted to the right by one key). This is recommended for people like the creator, who like having some extra keys to the left of their hand.

These keybinds are called "Elite" because they provide better control in-game, but are less familiar for most players.

Movement

  • Move Forward - E
  • Move Back - D
  • Strafe Left - S
  • Strafe Right - F
  • Jump - Space
  • Crouch - A

Actions

  • Fire - Left Mouse
  • Alt-Fire - Right Mouse
  • Offhand Attack - W
  • Offhand Ability - Middle Mouse
  • Offhand Ability (alt.) - Shift
  • Interact - R
  • Super Form - Y
  • Taunt - T

Weapons

  • Last Used Weapon - Q
  • Previous Weapon - Scroll Up
  • Next Weapon - Scroll Down
  • Melee - 1
  • Weapon 1 - 2
  • Weapon 2 - 3
  • Weapon 3 - 4
  • Weapon 4 - 5

Controller

Familiar

The default controller bindings, based on using the A button to jump.

Note that this uses Xbox face button, bumper and trigger names and PlayStation stick names, because LS/RS is ambiguous and L3/R3 makes it clear that you have to press the sticks.

Movement

  • Move Around - Left Stick
  • Look Around - Right Stick
  • Jump - A
  • Crouch - B

Actions

  • Fire - RT
  • Alt-Fire - LT
  • Offhand Attack - X
  • Offhand Ability - L3
  • Offhand Ability (alt.) - N/A
  • Interact - R3
  • Super Form - Up
  • Taunt - Down

Weapons

  • Last Used Weapon - Y
  • Previous Weapon - LB
  • Next Weapon - RB

Elite

Controller bindings based on using LB to jump. Surprisingly, these are not the bindings the creator uses, he uses Familiar bindings when he uses a controller, though he typically doesn't use a controller.

Movement

  • Move Around - Left Stick
  • Look Around - Right Stick
  • Jump - LB
  • Crouch - RB

Actions

  • Fire - RT
  • Alt-Fire - LT
  • Offhand Attack - X
  • Offhand Ability - L3
  • Offhand Ability (alt.) - B
  • Interact - A
  • Super Form - Y
  • Taunt - Down

Weapons

  • Last Used Weapon - Up
  • Previous Weapon - Left
  • Next Weapon - Right
* Alternate input for Offhand Ability, bound to the +speed binding
