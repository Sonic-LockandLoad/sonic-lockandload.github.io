Skills and weapons

Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic is a blend of Sonic: Lock & Load's Modern and Classic Sonic, with super high mobility, but with an air of classic DOOM-ness.

He can blast through the levels much faster than Doomguy would be able to, and he doesn't even need a rocket-powered wheelchair to do it!

A lot of his weapons and movement skills can be tied together for high speed and fast action gameplay.

Skills

  • Kick - Offhand Attack
    Sonic can kick enemies at any time.
  • Spin Dash - Offhand Ability on the ground
    Sonic can rev up in ball form and launch himself towards any enemies! Despite being an Offhand Ability, it DOES deal damage.
  • Drop Dash - Offhand Ability in the air
    Sonic can charge up a Spin Dash mid-air and launch as soon as he touches the ground. It doesn't go as far as Spin Dash, but it's still pretty useful.

Weapons

  • Caliburn - Sonic's personal talking sword from a dimension of stories.
    • Sword Swing - Fire
      Swing Caliburn from side to side. Hold down Fire to continuously swing. Attacks multiple times in a single hit.
    • Boost - Alt-Fire
      Make Sonic zoom forward at high speed. The longer you hold, the faster it'll go. Press Jump to perform a Boost Jump, which preserves the Boost's momentum and gives Sonic a height boost. Can be held mid-air, unlike its S:L&L counterpart.
  • Speedforce Pistol - A railshot pistol that can send Sonic flying back quite a bit.
    • Speedforce Burst - Fire
      Fire a weak railgun shot.
    • Charged Burst - Fire
      Fire a strong railgun shot.
  • Speedy Shotgun - An automatic shotgun with a burst fire.
    • Speedy Shot - Fire
      Fire a rapid shotgun blast.
  • Eclipse Blaster - An enhanced plasma rifle that has the ability to heal.
    • Eclipse Shot - Fire
      Fire a stream of plasma that deals continuous damage to enemies.
    • Chaos Restoration - Alt-Fire
      The Eclipse Blaster can heal Sonic. It doesn't waste as much Energy as the S:L&L version, so go nuts.
  • Rocket Stream - A rapid fire rocket launcher that can be used for rocket jumping.
    • Stream Launch - Fire
      Fire a stream of rockets that launch targets far, including Sonic. Sonic takes less damage from his own rockets, so you can get away with rocket jumping.
