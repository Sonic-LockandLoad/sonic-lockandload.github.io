Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Sonic: Lock & Load related at all to Sonic Robo Blast 2?
    • No - SRB2 is a completely different project from Sonic: Lock & Load. SRB2 is a Sonic fan game that was made in the image of the original Genesis games, while Sonic: Lock and Load is a first person shooter based on the Sonic the Hedgehog series, with an attempt to blend elements of Sonic and DOOM together.
  • How do I play this thing?
    • You need GZDoom and a copy of DOOM. If you don't want to buy DOOM, Freedoom will work just as well. It's recommended you use either DOOM II or Freedoom: Phase 2 if you want to play the Side Levels or Tutorials.
  • Why is Classic Sonic in the game?
    • I wanted to bring back the original style from Sonic the Hedgehog in DOOM, Sonic: Lock & Load's predecessor. He's not just a Sonic clone -- he's his own character.
  • Where can I ask about or complain about the mod?
    • You can leave an issue on the GitHub repo.
