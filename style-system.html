Style System

Is your memory card set correctly?
This page describes a feature massively expanded in Sonic: Lock & Load version v1.4.1, which is a future release.
Proceed with caution and keep your expectations set low.

The style system, taking after the style system in games like Devil May Cry and ULTRAKILL, is integral to obtaining score and achieving high ranks in Sonic: Lock & Load.

Style Ranks

The higher rank you get, the faster you'll regenerate Chaos Energy (if it's enabled in the options), and the faster your style points will decay.

Style Bonuses

During gameplay, when defeating enemies, you'll be awarded style bonuses by doing specific things, which will boost your style points and help you attain higher Style Ranks.

Bonuses marked with * will only appear in the messages list when an enemy is killed, but the bonus still applies according to the description.

Some bonuses add to your style points - others will multiply the bonus you get from dealing damage in the first place.
